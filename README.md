# Nemours Pediatric Market Opportunity Analysis

## Project Overview

As part of my senior capstone project at the University of Delaware, I partnered with Nemours Children's Health to identify high-potential geographic markets for pediatric healthcare outreach and expansion. The goal was to determine which ZIP codes within the Delaware Valley region demonstrated strong demographic and socioeconomic characteristics for pediatric services but were currently underpenetrated by Nemours.

By combining Census Bureau data, patient utilization data, and community asset information, our team developed a data-driven framework to help healthcare leaders prioritize outreach efforts, community partnerships, and future growth opportunities.

---

## Business Problem

Healthcare organizations must strategically allocate marketing resources and expansion efforts to maximize patient reach and improve access to care.

Nemours sought to answer several key questions:

* Which ZIP codes have the highest pediatric growth potential?
* Where are underserved communities located?
* Which demographic and socioeconomic factors influence patient utilization?
* What community organizations could serve as potential outreach and partnership opportunities?

Our objective was to transform large, fragmented datasets into actionable insights that would support data-driven decision-making.

## Key Findings

* Identified high-opportunity ZIP codes with strong demographic indicators but below-average patient penetration.
* Determined that commuting patterns and geographic accessibility were among the strongest predictors of market penetration.
* Developed a market opportunity scoring system that enabled stakeholders to prioritize outreach efforts and community partnerships.
* Delivered actionable recommendations for expanding pediatric healthcare access and increasing community engagement.
